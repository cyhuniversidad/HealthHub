Accessibility Statement - HealthHub

Commitment to Accessibility

At HealthHub, we are committed to ensuring the accessibility of our website for everyone, regardless of their abilities. We strive to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at level A and with the Royal Decree 1112/2018.

Accessibility Features Implemented

  • Language identification: All pages of our site are correctly tagged with the language attribute to facilitate navigation with screen readers.
  • Alt text: Images are provided with descriptive alt text to ensure that visual content is accessible to people with visual impairments.
  • Clear navigation: We have implemented a site map to make it easier to navigate all the content available on HealthHub.
  • Semantic structure: We use HTML5 semantic tags to ensure that the content structure is understandable to assistive technologies.
  • Adequate contrast: The site design is optimized to ensure adequate contrast, improving content readability for people with low vision.
  • Keyboard access: All interactive elements are accessible via the keyboard for users with motor disabilities.

Feedback Mechanisms

If you experience difficulties accessing any part of our website or would like to provide feedback on accessibility, feel free to contact us through the Customer Help Form.

Compliance Levels

This website mostly complies with level A of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, although we are still working to improve certain elements to ensure full accessibility.

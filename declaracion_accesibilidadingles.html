Accessibility Statement - HealthHub

Commitment to Accessibility

At HealthHub, we are committed to ensuring the accessibility of our website for everyone, regardless of their abilities. We strive to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at level A and with Royal Decree 1112/2018.

Accessibility Features Implemented

  • Language Identification: All our pages are properly tagged with a language attribute to facilitate navigation with screen readers.
  • Alt Text: Images include descriptive alternative text to ensure that visual content is accessible to individuals with visual impairments.
  • Clear Navigation: We have implemented a sitemap to facilitate navigation through all the content available on HealthHub.
  • Semantic Structure: We use semantic HTML5 tags to ensure that the content structure is understandable for assistive technologies.
  • Proper Contrast: The site's design is optimized to ensure adequate contrast, improving content readability for individuals with low vision.
  • Keyboard Access: All interactive elements are keyboard-accessible for users with motor disabilities.

Feedback Mechanisms

If you experience any difficulties accessing any part of our website or wish to provide feedback on accessibility, please contact us through the Customer Assistance Form.

Conformance Levels

This website is largely compliant with the A level of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, although we are working on improving certain elements to ensure full accessibility.
